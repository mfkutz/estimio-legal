Estimio · Frequently asked questions

Getting started
Tap the Quotes tab at the bottom, then tap the + button. Fill in the client, add the items or services you want to include, set the validity date, and tap Save. Your quote is ready to share.
Go to the Clients tab and tap +. Enter the client's name and any contact details you want to save. Once created, the client will be available when creating quotes.
Go to the Services tab and tap +. Add a name, description, and price. Saved services can be quickly added to any quote without having to type them again each time.
Go to Settings (top right corner of the Stats screen) and tap Business name or Country. Selecting a country will automatically set the matching currency.

Account
Go to Settings → Reset password. We'll send a reset link to your email address. If you signed up with Google, this option is not available — your password is managed by your Google account.
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete account. You'll be asked to type your email to confirm. This permanently deletes all your quotes, clients, services, and profile. This action cannot be undone.

Subscription
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel, upgrade, or view your billing details. Cancelling will keep your access active until the end of the current billing period.
