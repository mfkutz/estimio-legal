Privacy Policy

Estimio · Last updated: April 2025

At Estimio, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data.

1. Who we are

Estimio is a mobile application for creating and managing quotes, clients, and services. It is independently developed and operated.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and email address (when you sign up)
  • Business name, country, and currency
  • Client data you add to the app (name, phone, email, address)
  • Quotes and services you create

Information collected automatically:

  • Anonymous usage data (screens visited, actions taken) to improve the app
  • Technical information for error diagnosis (device type, OS version)

3. How we use your information

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Authenticate your account and protect your security
  • Improve the app experience through usage analytics
  • Diagnose and fix technical errors
  • Manage your subscription (if applicable)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Third-party services

Estimio uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase User authentication and data storage — View policy
  • Google Sign-In Sign in with Google account — View policy
  • RevenueCat Subscription and in-app payment management — View policy
  • Mixpanel Anonymous usage analytics — View policy
  • Sentry Error monitoring and diagnosis — View policy
  • Expo App updates — View policy

5. Storage and security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Google Cloud) servers, protected with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement access controls so only you can read and modify your information.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account from the app (Settings → Danger Zone → Delete account), we will permanently delete all your data: quotes, clients, services, and profile. This process is irreversible.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your information at any time from within the app
  • Correct incorrect data from the Settings section
  • Delete your account and all your data permanently
  • Request information about how your data is used

8. Children

Estimio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us to have that information removed.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised date. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: privacy@estimio.app
